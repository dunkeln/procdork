`main.mart_chat_summary` is the available chat summary mart. It contains four nullable `BIGINT` count metrics—`session_count`, `message_count`, `event_count`, and `cited_source_count`—plus nullable `TIMESTAMP WITH TIME ZONE` fields `first_session_at` and `last_session_at`.
